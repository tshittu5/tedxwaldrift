What We Are

We are a group of individuals who believe in the power of ideas and the impact they have on our society, that’s why we exist now in this part of the world. We come from different backgrounds such as music, radio, sport, and tech. We are so excited to be putting on the maiden edition of TEDxWaldrift 2023, and we can’t wait to shape this incredible community with you all.
